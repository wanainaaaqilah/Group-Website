Pet adoption is the process of taking responsibility for a pet that a previous owner has abandoned or released to a shelter or rescue organization. Common sources for adoptable pets are animal shelters and rescue groups. Apart from that, our websites has been establish for people aware with the cat adoption because everywhere we go there are too many stray cats. As a human being, we need to responsible and care the cats in order to they get a good and wonderful life.

CHARACTERISTIC FOR ADOPTERS

-MUST BE LOVELY PERSON EITHER BOY OR GIRLS

-THE ADOPTERS NEED TO BRING THE CAT TO THE CLINIC ONCE A MONTH

RULES

25 years old and above

In addition to insurance liabilities, rescues don't like to adopt to young people because they don't usually know where they'll be in 5 years. If you go to college, you won't be able to take your pet with you. If you move to a no-pets building, you'll need to find a new home for the animal

The ideal age to adopt a kitten is ideally, kittens should go to their new home around 12 weeks of age. While some kittens can go home earlier, the closer you wait until 12 or 13 weeks, the better off the kitten will be.

When the adopters want to buy our cat, they need to pay with food instead of money because the food can use and give to others cat adoption.
